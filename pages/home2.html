FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries about ordering, tastings, or delivery? Explore our common answers.

How far in advance should I book my custom cake?

We recommend commissioning your custom cake 3 to 6 months in advance. Because we prioritize attention to detail, we accept a limited number of orders per week to ensure every creation is a flawless sculptural work of art.

Do you host flavor tasting consultations?

Yes, we host private tasting sessions for custom design commissions exceeding $500. During the session, you will explore signature flavor pairings and sketch layout options with Sarah Jenkins.

Can you accommodate organic or dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. We specialize in luxury vegan, gluten-free, eggless, and nut-free adaptations of our classic recipe tiers, maintaining the exact visual scale and texture quality of our main collection.

What is your white-glove setup and delivery fee?

White-glove delivery and on-site assembly are custom calculated based on travel distance and cake size. We transport all creations in temperature-controlled vans to guarantee they arrive in pristine condition.
