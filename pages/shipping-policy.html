Shipping & Delivery Policy

We take great care in delivering our cakes to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

1. Delivery Zones

We currently deliver within a 50-mile radius of our Sugar City studio. Delivery fees are calculated at checkout based on distance.

2. Handling

All cakes are transported in temperature-controlled vehicles. Once delivered, please follow the storage instructions provided with your order.

3. Pickup

Complimentary studio pickup is available Tuesday through Saturday during our normal business hours.
