How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

For custom wedding cakes, we recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance. For other custom celebration cakes, 2-4 weeks is usually sufficient. However, we do occasionally have last-minute openings, so it's always worth asking!

Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a dedicated range of delicious vegan and gluten-free sponge flavors. While we take every precaution to avoid cross-contamination, please note that our studio handles nuts, gluten, and dairy.

Can you deliver to my venue?

Absolutely. We offer temperature-controlled delivery within a 50-mile radius of our studio. Delivery fees are calculated based on distance. For tiered wedding cakes, we highly recommend our professional delivery and set-up service.

How should I store my cake after I get it home?

Most of our cakes are best enjoyed at room temperature. If your event is on the same day, keep the cake in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. If you need to store it overnight, keep it refrigerated but take it out 2-3 hours before serving to let the sponge and buttercream soften.

Do you offer cake tastings?

Yes! We offer "Taster Boxes" for wedding inquiries which can be ordered through our shop. We also host monthly tasting events at our studio where you can sample our most popular flavors.
