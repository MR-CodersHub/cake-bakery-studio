Our Story

Born from Passion,
Refined by Artistry

What began in a small boutique kitchen with a single rolling pin and a vision for French patisserie has evolved into the city's premier destination for luxury cake design.

Cakeology was founded on the belief that a cake should be more than just a dessert—it should be a sculpture that captures the essence of a moment. Our journey has been one of continuous refinement, sourcing the world's most exquisite ingredients to ensure that every bite matches the visual splendor of our creations.

Today, our studio is a playground for innovation, where traditional techniques meet contemporary aesthetics to serve a clientele that appreciates the finer details of celebration.

The Founder

Isabella Rose

"My philosophy is simple: perfection is found in the intersection of flavor and form. I don't just bake cakes; I design experiences that linger in the memory long after the last crumb is gone."

With a background in fine arts and professional training at the Le Cordon Bleu, Isabella brings a unique perspective to the world of pastry. Her signature style—a blend of ethereal botanical details and modern structural minimalism—has redefined luxury baking for a new generation of celebrants.
