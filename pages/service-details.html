Luxury Wedding Cake Design

Your wedding cake is more than just a dessert; it's a centerpiece of your celebration and a reflection of your unique love story. At Cakeology, we specialize in creating breathtaking multi-tiered wedding cakes that combine architectural precision with delicate artistry.

What's Included:

  • Personal design consultation and sketching session.
  • Cake tasting box with 6 signature flavors.
  • On-site setup and professional assembly at your venue.
  • Coordination with your florist for fresh flower integration.
  • Complimentary 1st anniversary mini-cake.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 6-9 months in advance for wedding season, as we only take a limited number of commissions per week.
Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?
Yes, we offer a range of delicious vegan and gluten-free cake flavors that maintain our signature premium quality.
Do you deliver to out-of-city venues?
Yes, we provide delivery within a 100-mile radius of our studio. Delivery fees are calculated based on distance.
