Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your sweet treats. Due to the perishable nature of our products, our refund policy is as follows:

1. Cancellations

Orders cancelled more than 7 days before the delivery date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made within 7 days may be subject to a cancellation fee.

2. Satisfaction

If you are unsatisfied with your order, please contact us immediately. We will do our best to make it right, which may include a partial credit or replacement.
